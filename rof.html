Week 2: Ring of Fire

The Concept

So one of my fav drinking games to play has to be a game called "Ring of Fire" and it has to be one of the most popular games played by my friends and I.

The game concept is pretty simple. A deck of cards are placed round a cup, Players must then take it in turns to draw a card from the circle, each card has diffrent rules attatched, these rules are normally a drinking related challange (One of the parts of the spec was to include a rules list in the APP as sometimes the rules can be hard to remember)
A few times over the last few months I have said I could easily build this game out as an phone app meaning that we no longer need to seek out a pack of cards everytime we play.
